Tic tac toe

You are X playing against the computer O. To win the game, you need to set your "X" in specific patterns which can be in a row, line, column, or curve.

The Dice

This is a very easy game to play. You just need to click on the Throw the Dice button, then the device will give you the result of your throw and the computer's throw as well. Whoever gets 15 points first will win the game.
